Xiaomi 11i 5G

The Xiaomi 11i 5G is a smartphone that offers a blend of performance, camera capabilities, and design. It features a large AMOLED display with a high refresh rate, making it ideal for media consumption and gaming . Powered by a robust processor, it ensures smooth multitasking and efficient performance for everyday tasks. The device boasts a versatile camera setup, allowing users to capture high-quality photos and videos in various conditions. With 5G connectivity, users can enjoy faster internet speeds and improved network reliability. The Xiaomi 11i 5G also comes with a sizable battery that supports fast charging, ensuring that users can stay connected throughout the day.

Key Features / Highlights

Display

The Xiaomi 11i 5G features a 6.67-inch AMOLED display with a resolution of 1080 x 2400 pixels and a 120Hz refresh rate, providing vibrant colors and smooth scrolling experiences.

Processor

It is powered by the MediaTek Dimensity 920 chipset, which ensures efficient performance and supports 5G connectivity.

RAM & Storage

The device comes with options for 6GB or 8GB of RAM and 128GB or 256GB of internal storage, allowing users to choose based on their needs.
